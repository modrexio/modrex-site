title: Launching the game

## Launch modded

Click **Launch** in the top bar to start PAYDAY 3 with all enabled mods active. Modrex passes your
configured launch options to the game and hands off to your launcher (Steam, Epic, or Xbox).

Make sure `-fileopenlog` is in your launch options - without it most mods won't load. See
[Installation](/docs/getting-started#launch-options) for details.

## Launch without mods

Click the dropdown arrow next to **Launch** and select **Launch without mods**. Modrex temporarily
renames your `~mods/` folder to `~mods.bak/`, moving it outside the `Paks/` directory that Unreal
Engine scans. The game starts with no mods at all.

When PAYDAY 3 closes, Modrex automatically detects this and restores your `~mods/` folder.

| ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Steam      | ![yes](https://img.shields.io/badge/Yes-brightgreen) | ![yes](https://img.shields.io/badge/Yes-brightgreen) |
| Epic Games | ![yes](https://img.shields.io/badge/Yes-brightgreen) | ![no](https://img.shields.io/badge/No-red)           |
| Xbox App   | ![yes](https://img.shields.io/badge/Yes-brightgreen) | ![no](https://img.shields.io/badge/No-red)           |

To override the detected launcher, go to **Settings - Launcher** and select one manually.
