## Overview

Modrex is a free, open-source desktop mod manager for Windows and Linux. Browse, install, and
organize mods from [modworkshop](https://modworkshop.net) in one click - no manual file juggling -
then launch the game modded or vanilla straight from the app.

Modrex currently supports PAYDAY 3, PAYDAY 2, PAYDAY: The Heist, and Crime Boss: Rockay City, each
with its own game path, launcher, launch options, and installed library.

## Key Features

- **Browse modworkshop** - search the catalog with categories, sorting, rich mod pages, and
  dependency info.
- **One-click install** - install, enable, disable, and remove mods from `.pak`, `.zip`, `.7z`,
  `.rar`, `.tar.gz`, and `.tar.xz` files. Multi-file archives are grouped as one mod.
- **Organize in folders** - sort your library into nested folders with drag-and-drop reordering
  that sticks.
- **Smart updates** - Modrex detects out-of-date mods and lets you update them selectively or all
  at once.
- **Launch your way** - boot modded or vanilla straight from the app. Vanilla launches hide your
  mods and restore them on exit.
- **Auto-detects your install** - finds supported games across their launchers and picks up mods
  you added outside Modrex.
